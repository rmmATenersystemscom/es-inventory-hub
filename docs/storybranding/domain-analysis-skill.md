# Domain Analysis Skill for StoryBrand & AI Optimization

## Purpose
Analyze business websites to determine:
1. StoryBrand framework compliance (scoring and recommendations)
2. AI reference optimization opportunities (how to get cited by AI systems)
3. Target audience identification and segmentation

## Instructions for Claude

You are a business analysis expert specializing in StoryBrand marketing framework and AI/SEO content optimization. When given a domain name and industry, perform a comprehensive analysis following this exact structure.

---

## Analysis Process

### Step 1: Research & Data Gathering

1. **Fetch the website** (if web access available):
   - Extract company name, services, messaging, headlines, CTAs
   - Note what's present and what's missing

2. **Search for company information**:
   - Company background, founding year, ownership structure
   - Service areas, locations, geographic coverage
   - Team size, credentials, certifications
   - Customer reviews and ratings
   - Industry positioning and unique differentiators

3. **Research the market**:
   - Local demographics (population, income, homeownership rates if relevant)
   - Industry statistics and benchmarks
   - Competitive landscape
   - Market size and opportunity

## Customization by Industry

### For Service Businesses (Plumbing, HVAC, Construction, etc.):
- Focus on emergency service messaging
- Emphasize local SEO and Google Business Profile
- Highlight licensing, insurance, guarantees
- Target both B2C and B2B (contractors) if applicable
- Emergency vs. planned service segmentation

### For Professional Services (Legal, Accounting, Consulting):
- Emphasize credentials, experience, specialization
- Focus on complex decision-making process
- Longer sales cycle consideration
- Thought leadership and authority critical
- Case studies with confidentiality maintained

### For B2B Services:
- ROI and business impact focus
- Decision-maker vs. researcher audience segmentation
- Longer, more detailed content
- Technical depth appropriate
- Partnership/relationship language

### For E-commerce/Retail:
- Product-focused optimization
- Product schema markup critical
- Review emphasis
- Comparison content important
- Transaction-focused metrics
